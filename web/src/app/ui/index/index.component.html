Make your profile more powerful

Make your Discord profile more powerful with these features.

  • See what your friends are watching

Retrieve information from your match that you are watching, includes elapsed time and networth.

  • Show off your heroes with your favorite skins

Different icons for each skin that your hero is wearing.

  • Show your skills of accumulating money in the game

Find information about accumulated gold, last hits and negations, available by hovering the mouse.

  • Show your items and your buffs

Different icons for each item or effect you have.

  • Modified map support*

Shows a different name for modified maps.

Works only on registered maps.
  • See your instructors

Your coach deserves a different presence too.

  • Multiple language support*

He says: 'Possum intellegere te'. Auto-detect your language.

Check if the desired translation is available.
